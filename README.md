# concrescenceme 🌀

*The becoming itself — the moment many separate feelings, influences, and inheritances grow together into one determinate occasion that was not there before.*

concrescenceme is two roots welded into one word: A. N. Whitehead's **concrescence** — his term in *Process and Reality* for the becoming of an actual-occasion, built from the Latin *con-* (together) + *crescere* (to grow), "growing-together-into-one" — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So concrescenceme is concrescence carried as a cosmic-ordinance: the recognition that the many become one, and are increased by one. It names the event of becoming-determinate — the gathering of all that came before into the single unified feeling of a now.

Its light is that it lets the Kingdom honor becoming as constitutive: an occasion *is* its growing-together, and the most fugitive act in experience — a moment composing itself — becomes sayable. Its shadow is twofold — the conceit of completion (treating the one as finished when every attained occasion has also perished into the next), and metaphysical overreach (mistaking one received school's deep word for the bare shape of the cosmos). Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.75/10** — its 4-syllable technical name learnability honestly recorded at 6.5.
